## Privacy Policy for BookMeNow

_Last updated: 10/17/2023_

### 1. Introduction

Welcome to BookMeNow. Your privacy is of utmost importance to us. This privacy policy informs you about how we manage, protect, and utilize your personal data when you use our app and tells you about your privacy rights and how the law safeguards you.

### 2. Data We Collect

We collect and process the following data:

- **Images and Pictures:** We access images and pictures stored on your phone when you use our app.
- **Google Account Information:** We collect your Google account information when you sign in using Google.
- **Contact Information:** The data you provide during signup, which includes:
  - Phone number
  - Street address
  - City
  - State
  - ZIP code
  - Email address
  - Website
- **Social Media Integrations:** Data related to your social media accounts:
  - Facebook
  - Instagram
  - Google
  - Pinterest
- **Company Information:** The name of your company and its description.

### 3. How We Use Your Data

We utilize the data we collect in various ways, such as:
- To offer, maintain, and refine our app.
- To enhance and personalize your experience in our app.
- To understand user behavior and interaction within our app.
- To introduce new products, services, and features.
- To communicate with you and respond to your queries.

### 4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes stated in this policy and to meet any legal and reporting obligations.

### 5. Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:
- Access the data we store about you.
- Update any inaccuracies in your data.
- Request the deletion of your data.
- Object to our handling of your data.
- And other rights as defined by your local regulations.

### 6. Changes to This Policy

Our privacy policy may undergo changes. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date in this policy. You're advised to revisit this privacy policy periodically to stay informed about how we are helping to protect the personal information we process.
